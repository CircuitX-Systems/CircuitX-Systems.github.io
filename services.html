Our Engineering Services

Specialized expertise to solve your toughest technology challenges.

Embedded Systems & Firmware

Firmware is the heart of any intelligent device. We develop robust, efficient, and reliable code for microcontrollers and embedded processors, ensuring your hardware performs securely and optimally.

  • Custom Firmware for ARM Cortex-M, ESP32, and other MCUs
  • Real-Time Operating System (RTOS) Integration
  • Bare-Metal Programming for maximum performance
  • Driver Development & Hardware Abstraction Layers (HAL)

IoT & Wireless Connectivity

We connect devices seamlessly. From local networks to global cellular connectivity, we design solutions that balance power efficiency, security, and data integrity.

  • Wi-Fi, Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), and Mesh Networking
  • LoRaWAN and other Low-Power WAN (LPWAN) technologies
  • Cellular integration (LTE-M, NB-IoT) for remote assets
  • Cloud communication protocols: MQTT, CoAP, and HTTP

Cloud & Backend Integration

Scalable, secure, and intelligent backend systems to process data from thousands of devices, giving you actionable insights and control over your IoT ecosystem.

  • Cloud architecture on AWS and Azure
  • Data processing pipelines and storage solutions
  • API development for web and mobile platforms
  • Device management and Over-The-Air (OTA) updates
