Our Engineering Services

Specialized expertise to solve your toughest technology challenges.

Embedded Systems & Firmware Development

The core of any intelligent device is its firmware. We specialize in writing robust, efficient, and reliable code for microcontrollers and embedded processors. Our expertise ensures your hardware performs optimally, securely, and exactly as intended.

  • Custom Firmware for ARM Cortex-M, ESP32, and other MCUs
  • Real-Time Operating System (RTOS) Integration
  • Bare-Metal Programming for maximum performance
  • Driver Development and Hardware Abstraction Layers (HAL)

IoT & Wireless Connectivity

We connect your devices to the world. Whether you need short-range communication or global cellular connectivity, we design and implement the right wireless strategy for your product's specific needs, focusing on power efficiency and data integrity.

  • Wi-Fi, Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), and Mesh Networking
  • LoRaWAN and other Low-Power WAN (LPWAN) technologies
  • Cellular (LTE-M, NB-IoT) integration for remote assets
  • Cloud communication protocols like MQTT and CoAP

Cloud & Backend Integration

An IoT solution is incomplete without a powerful cloud backend. We architect and build scalable server-side applications that can ingest, process, and analyze data from thousands of devices, providing you with actionable insights.

  • Cloud architecture on AWS and Microsoft Azure
  • Data processing pipelines and storage solutions
  • API development for web and mobile applications
  • Device management and over-the-air (OTA) update systems
